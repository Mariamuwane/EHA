Things to see

      Lazienki Palace, Warsaw

    The beautiful palace in the capital city of Warsaw, Poland. Its park is one of the oldest in the city. The Łazienki Park covers an area of 76 ha and its unique character and history is reflected in the landscape architecture (pavilions, sculptures, bridges, water cascades) and vegetation (domestic and foreign species of trees and shrubs). The presence of peacocks, pheasants and squirrels at Łazienki attracts tourists and locals.

      The Colosseum

    I wanted to visit this place since I saw the movie, The Gladiator. Thousands of years later, the amphitheatre is still standing. Located in Rome, Italy, the Colosseum is one of the most visited places in the world.

Places you can visit

  1. India

    2. It is the second most populous country in the world. India is notable for its religious diversity , being Hinduism , Sikhism , Islam , Christianity and Jainism the main and most popular religions in the country. Some of the most popular festivals in the country are: Diwali , Holi , Durga Puja , Eid ul-Fitr , Eid al-Adha , Christmas and Vesak. Indian cuisine features a heavy reliance on herbs and spices , with dishes often appealing to the subtle use of a dozen or more different seasonings.

  2. Chile

    3. Chile is a country in the western part of South America. It is located along a highly seismic and volcanic zone, part of the Pacific Ring of Fire, due to the subduction of the Nazca and Antarctic plates in the South American plate.As of 2012, 66.6% of Chilean population over 15 years of age claimed to adhere to the Roman Catholic church, a decrease from the 70%. Its places of natural beauty situated in the extreme zones of the country: San Pedro de Atacama, in the north, is very popular with foreign tourists who arrive to admire the Incaic architecture, the altiplano lakes, and the Valley of the Moon. In Putre, also in the north, there is the Chungará Lake, as well as the Parinacota and the Pomerape volcanoes, with altitudes of 6,348 m and 6,282 m, respectively. Throughout the central Andes there are many ski resorts of international repute, including Portillo, Valle Nevado and Termas de Chillán.
